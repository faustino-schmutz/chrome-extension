Privacy Policy for Right Click Unlocker Pro

Last updated: January 2025

Overview

Right Click Unlocker Pro ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome extension handles information.

Information We Do NOT Collect

Right Click Unlocker Pro does not collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

  • Personal information (name, email, address, etc.)
  • Browsing history or website data
  • Passwords or login credentials
  • Financial information
  • Location data
  • Device information
  • Analytics or tracking data

Local Storage Only

The extension stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Extension enabled/disabled state
  • Premium license status
  • Site whitelist/blacklist preferences (Premium feature)
  • User interface preferences

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any servers.

Permissions Explanation

Our extension requests the following Chrome permissions:

  • activeTab: To restore right-click functionality on the current tab
  • storage: To save your preferences locally on your device
  • tabs: To manage extension state across browser tabs

Third-Party Services

Right Click Unlocker Pro does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or external APIs.

Payment Processing

Payments are processed entirely through the Chrome Web Store using Google's secure payment system. We do not have access to your payment information.

Data Security

Since we don't collect or transmit any data, there are no data security concerns. All preferences are stored locally using Chrome's secure storage APIs.

Children's Privacy

Our extension does not collect any information from users of any age, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

Open Source

Our extension code is available for review on GitHub, demonstrating our commitment to transparency and privacy.
